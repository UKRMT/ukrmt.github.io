Who We Are

We are a University of Kentucky student organization dedicated to STEM involvement of the student body.

What We Do

Design and develop a robotic mining vehicle to compete at NASA's annual robotic mining competition hosted at Kennedy Space Center.

The Competition

Wireless robots are given 10 minute trials to mine as much simulated Mars dirt as possible. Judged on amount of dirt mined, network throughput and level of autonomy achieved while mining.
